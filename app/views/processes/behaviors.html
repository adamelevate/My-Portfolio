Here are two workflows depicting potential customer behaviors for a toy company's kiosk.

When I saw the original flow (upper) I had some concerns about the amount of work a customer would have to do to get to the fun part. Since there is no other product like this out there, their motivation might not be able to overcome the work needed.

That's what led me to create the lower flow. However, building it would have required the client to change their business model and that wasn't on the table.
