Leadership

I've always been very empathetic. I can understand people, put myself in their shoes and work out their position from their perspective. I'm not psychic, I'm just observant.

I'm passionate about helping people, it's the only thing in life that is truly rewarding. Sometimes, people who need help, can't see how to improve their situation. That's where I come in.

I think differently. I turn problems on their head to see what change falls out of their pockets. I'm always asking "Why, How, What" , reframing a problem to get a better angle and burning the midnight oil to make the world a better place.

My Guiding Principles

I have some phrases that keep me level-headed and focused on doing what I do best:

  • Do the right thing, it's never too late.
  • Always know why. How else will you know what to do?
  • Think before you act, it matters.
  • Too Easy? You might have missed something.
  • Too Hard? You might be doing it wrong.
  • Everyone works for you, so ask for their support.
  • You work for everyone, so ask if they need support.
  • Nothing is beneath you, every job is important.
