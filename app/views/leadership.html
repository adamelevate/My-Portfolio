Leadership

I am passionate about helping people. Sometimes people who need help don't know their situation can actually be improved. That's where I come in.

I've always been very empathetic. I can understand people, put myself in their shoes and work out their position from their perspective. I'm not psychic, I'm just observant. I believe that helping people is the only thing in life that is truly rewarding.

I love problem solving, that's why I wake up everyday. Since no person can do anything great alone, I tend to find other passionate people who share the cause. I love inspiring those around me to do something meaningful and worthwhile.

I'm a huge fan of Simon Sinek's "Why, How, What" TED talk. I have always thought about life this way, asking why for everything. I've learned so much just asking the simple question: "Why?". I happen to be interested in how everything works, and when dealing with any problem, I work through it in this manner.

My Guiding Principles

I have some phrases that keep me level-headed and focused on doing what I do best.

  • Do the right thing, it's never too late.
  • Always know why. How else will you know what to do?
  • Work with people who care.
  • Think before you act, it matters.
  • Too Easy? You might have missed something.
  • Too Hard? You might be doing it wrong.
  • Everyone works for you, so ask for their support.
  • You work for everyone, so ask if they need support.
  • Nothing is beneath you, every job is important.
